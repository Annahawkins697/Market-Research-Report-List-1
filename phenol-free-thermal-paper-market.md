<p><p>The phenol-free thermal paper market is categorized into two main types: top coating and no top coating. Top-coated thermal paper features a protective layer that enhances resistance to moisture, chemicals, and physical wear, making it ideal for demanding environments. Conversely, no top coating thermal paper lacks this protective layer, typically resulting in lower costs and suitability for less critical applications. Both types cater to diverse industries, promoting safety and environmental sustainability by eliminating harmful phenolic compounds.</p></p>
