<p><p>The reusable bronchoscopes market is divided into two main types: rigid bronchoscopes and flexible bronchoscopes. Rigid bronchoscopes, characterized by their solid structure, are used for direct visualization and intervention in the airway. They offer stability and improved access but are limited in maneuverability. In contrast, flexible bronchoscopes provide greater adaptability and visualization capabilities, allowing for navigating complex airway anatomies. They are widely utilized in diagnostic and therapeutic procedures due to their versatility and patient comfort.</p></p>
