<p><p>The Vehicle Positioning Module Market encompasses two primary types: centimeter-level and decimeter-level positioning. Centimeter-level positioning offers extremely high accuracy, suitable for applications requiring precise navigation, such as autonomous vehicles and advanced robotics. In contrast, decimeter-level positioning provides slightly lower precision, adequate for less critical applications like fleet management and logistics. Both types utilize advanced technologies such as GNSS and RTK to enhance vehicular positioning and ensure reliable, real-time data for various transportation and navigation needs.</p></p>
