<p><p>Radial piston pumps are classified into two main types: single-stage and multi-stage pumps. Single-stage pumps consist of one set of pistons and provide a consistent flow at a moderate pressure, making them suitable for applications requiring straightforward operation. In contrast, multi-stage pumps feature multiple sets of pistons, allowing them to achieve higher pressures and greater flow rates. This makes them ideal for high-demand applications, such as hydraulic systems, where efficiency and power are crucial.</p></p>
