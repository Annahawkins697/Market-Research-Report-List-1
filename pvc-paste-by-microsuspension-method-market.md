<p><strong>The PVC Paste by Microsuspension Method Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>

<p><p>The PVC Paste market using the microsuspension method includes two primary types: homopolymeric PVC paste and co-polymeric PVC paste. Homopolymeric PVC paste is derived from a single type of vinyl chloride monomer, offering excellent stability and rigid properties, making it suitable for various applications like coatings and adhesives. In contrast, co-polymeric PVC paste is created by polymerizing two different monomers, resulting in enhanced flexibility and impact resistance, ideal for specialized applications in films and flexible products.</p></p>
