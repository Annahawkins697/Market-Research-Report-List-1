<p><p>The food preservation additive market is primarily categorized into natural and artificial additives. In contrast, artificial additives are chemically synthesized ingredients designed to enhance shelf life, flavor, and appearance, such as synthetic preservatives and colorants. Both types play crucial roles in extending the freshness of food products, though consumer preferences increasingly lean toward natural options for perceived health benefits.</p></p>
