<p><ul><li>Bulk Polymerization</li><li>Suspension Polymerization</li><li>Solution Polymerization</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The azo-initiator market plays a crucial role in various polymerization techniques, including bulk, suspension, and solution polymerization. In bulk polymerization, azo-initiators facilitate the rapid formation of polymers directly from monomers, enhancing yield and efficiency. Suspension polymerization utilizes these initiators to create stable polymer beads in a continuous medium, ensuring uniform particle size. Solution polymerization relies on azo-initiators to generate polymers in a solvent, providing control over molecular weight and viscosity, thus catering to diverse industrial applications.</p></p>
