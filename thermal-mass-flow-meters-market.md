<p><p>Thermal mass flow meters are precision instruments used to measure the mass flow rate of gases based on the principles of thermal conductivity. They operate by employing a heated element that, when immersed in a gas flow, experiences cooling that is proportional to the mass flow rate. This technology is valued for its accuracy, particularly in low-flow applications, and is commonly utilized in industries such as oil and gas, chemical processing, and HVAC systems.</p><p>The Thermal Mass Flow Meters Market is anticipated to experience significant growth, projected to expand at a CAGR of 9.70% during the forecast period.
