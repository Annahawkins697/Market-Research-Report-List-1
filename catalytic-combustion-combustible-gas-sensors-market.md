<p><p>Catalytic combustion combustible gas sensors are essential for detecting flammable gases by utilizing catalytic reactions. The market is segmented into two primary types: defusion and inhalation. </p><p>Defusion sensors rely on passive gas diffusion through a protective layer to reach the catalytic element, allowing for continuous monitoring. Inhalation sensors actively draw gas samples into the sensor chamber, providing faster response times and improved detection accuracy. Both technologies play crucial roles in safety applications across industries, ensuring effective gas monitoring and hazard prevention.</p></p>
