<p><p>Spray dried milk powder is a dehydrated dairy product made by rapidly drying liquid milk through a spray drying process. This method involves atomizing the liquid milk into fine droplets and exposing them to heated air, resulting in the evaporation of moisture and the formation of a powder. Spray dried milk powder is used in a variety of applications, including food and beverage products, infant formulas, and nutritional supplements, due to its extended shelf life, easy reconstitution, and concentrated nutritional profile.</p><p>The Spray Dried Milk Powder Market is poised for significant growth, expected to expand at a CAGR of 8.00% during the forecast period. Additionally, the growing trend of using milk powder in various sectors such as bakery, confectionery, and dairy industries further propels market development. Skimmed milk powder, on the other hand, has reduced fat content, providing a lighter alternative that is often used in health-conscious food products, dairy substitutes, and infant formulas.
