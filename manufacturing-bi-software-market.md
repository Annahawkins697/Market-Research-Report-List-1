<p><p>Manufacturing BI (Business Intelligence) software is categorized into two primary types: cloud-based and on-premises solutions. In contrast, on-premises BI software is installed locally on a company’s hardware, providing greater control over data security and customization, suited for organizations with strict compliance requirements or existing infrastructure investments.</p></p>
