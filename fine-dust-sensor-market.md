<p><p>The Fine Dust Sensor market is categorized based on the particulate matter (PM) sizes detected, primarily PM10 and PM2.5. Sensors that output PM10 measure particles with a diameter of 10 micrometers or less, which can include dust, pollen, and mold spores. In contrast, PM2.5 sensors focus on finer particles, 2.5 micrometers or smaller, often linked to health risks due to their ability to penetrate deep into the lungs. Both types are essential for air quality monitoring and pollution control.</p></p>
