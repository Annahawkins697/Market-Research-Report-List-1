<p><p>The automobile gear oils market is categorized into three primary types: mineral oil, synthetic oil, and semi-synthetic oil. Mineral oil is derived from refining crude oil and is widely used for its affordability, but offers lower performance under extreme conditions. Synthetic oil is engineered for high performance, providing superior lubrication, heat resistance, and extended service life. Semi-synthetic oil combines both mineral and synthetic properties, offering better performance than mineral oils at a more economical price than full synthetics, catering to diverse consumer needs.</p></p>
