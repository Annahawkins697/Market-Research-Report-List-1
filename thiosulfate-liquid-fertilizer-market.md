<p><p>The Thiosulfate Liquid Fertilizer market is primarily segmented into two types: Ammonium Thiosulfate (ATS) and Potassium Thiosulfate (PTS). ATS is mainly used for nitrogen and sulfur nutrition in crops, promoting healthy growth and enhancing yield. PTS, on the other hand, provides potassium and sulfur, crucial for improved plant vigor and resistance to stress. The choice between ATS and PTS depends on specific crop needs and soil conditions, making them essential for targeted agricultural practices.</p></p>
