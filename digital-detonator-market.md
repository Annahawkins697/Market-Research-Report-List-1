<p><p>The digital detonator market is primarily categorized into two types: instantaneous electric detonators and delay electric detonators. Instantaneous electric detonators are designed to initiate explosions without any delay, providing immediate detonation for precise applications. In contrast, delay electric detonators allow controlled timing between consecutive detonations, enabling safer and more efficient blasting sequences. This differentiation in functionality suits various industries, including mining, construction, and demolition, each requiring specific timing and precision for optimal performance and safety in explosive operations.</p></p>
