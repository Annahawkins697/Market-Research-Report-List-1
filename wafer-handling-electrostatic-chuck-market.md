<p><strong>The Wafer Handling Electrostatic Chuck Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Coulomb Type Electrostatic Chuck</li><li>Johnsen-Rahbek (JR) Type Electrostatic Chuck</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Wafer Handling Electrostatic Chuck market primarily includes two types: Coulomb Type Electrostatic Chucks and Johnsen-Rahbek (JR) Type Electrostatic Chucks. Coulomb Type Chucks utilize electrostatic forces to hold wafers without mechanical contact, providing high stability and minimal particle contamination. In contrast, JR Type Chucks operate on a different principle, using induced charge to enhance wafer adhesion while allowing for precise alignment and handling at various temperatures. Both types are crucial for efficient semiconductor manufacturing.</p></p>
