<p><p>The surgical laser optical fiber market is divided into two main types: disposable and reusable fibers. Disposable optical fibers are designed for single-use, ensuring sterility and eliminating the risk of cross-contamination during surgical procedures. They offer convenience but can lead to higher costs over time. In contrast, reusable optical fibers are built for multiple uses, allowing for cost efficiency in the long run, but require proper sterilization and maintenance to ensure performance and safety in surgical applications.</p></p>
