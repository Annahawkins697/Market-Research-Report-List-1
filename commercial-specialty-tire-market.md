<p><ul><li>Radial Tires</li><li>Bias (Crossply) Tires</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The commercial specialty tire market primarily consists of two types: radial tires and bias (crossply) tires. Radial tires feature flexible sidewalls and belts that provide improved traction, stability, and fuel efficiency, making them ideal for heavy-duty applications. In contrast, bias tires have reinforced layers that cross each other, offering robust construction and durability, suitable for tough terrains. Both types serve specific needs in industries such as agriculture, construction, and mining, catering to varying load requirements and operational conditions.</p></p>
