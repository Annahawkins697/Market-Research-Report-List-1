<p><p>The B2B returnable packaging market includes Intermediate Bulk Containers (IBCs) and Flexible Intermediate Bulk Containers (FIBCs). IBCs are rigid, large containers used for transporting bulk liquids and solids, offering durability and reusability, while FIBCs, often made from woven fabric, are lightweight and flexible, designed for dry bulk materials. Both types facilitate efficient logistics and reduce waste, making them ideal for industries like chemicals, food, and pharmaceuticals. Their returnable nature enhances sustainability and cost-effectiveness in supply chains.</p></p>
