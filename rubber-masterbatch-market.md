<p><p>The Rubber Masterbatch market comprises several types based on the rubber type used. EPDM-based masterbatch, known for excellent weather resistance, is ideal for outdoor applications. SBR-based masterbatch, valued for its versatility and cost-effectiveness, is widely used in tires and general rubber products. NBR-based masterbatch offers superior oil and chemical resistance, making it suitable for automotive and industrial applications. Finally, natural rubber-based masterbatch boasts high elasticity and resilience, commonly used in various everyday products and components.</p></p>
