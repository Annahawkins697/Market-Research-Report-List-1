<p><p>The hot swap voltage controllers market comprises three main types: High Voltage Hot Swap Controllers, Low Voltage Hot Swap Controllers, and PCI Hot Swap Controllers. High voltage controllers manage devices operating above typical voltage levels, ensuring safe insertion and removal without downtime. Low voltage controllers are designed for applications with lower voltage requirements, protecting sensitive components during swapping. PCI Hot Swap Controllers specifically cater to peripheral component interconnect systems, facilitating the hot swapping of PCI cards while maintaining system stability and performance.</p></p>
