<p><p>The 3D log scanner market encompasses various technologies, prominently laser and X-ray scanning. Laser scanners utilize light beams to capture the external dimensions and surface qualities of logs, providing high-resolution data for efficient processing. X-ray scanners, on the other hand, penetrate the wood to assess internal structures and defects, offering insights into density and quality. Both technologies serve industries such as lumber milling and forestry management, enhancing operational efficiency and accuracy in log assessment and utilization.</p></p>
