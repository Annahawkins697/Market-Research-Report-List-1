<p><p>The infrared heat lamp market encompasses three primary types: Near Infrared, Medium Infrared, and Far Infrared. Near Infrared lamps emit short wavelengths, providing intense heat and quick warming, ideal for therapeutic use and industrial applications. Medium Infrared offers a balance, suitable for heating larger areas and used in heating pads and saunas. Far Infrared emits longer wavelengths, penetrating deeper into the skin, commonly utilized in wellness treatments and infrared saunas for relaxation and detoxification purposes.</p></p>
