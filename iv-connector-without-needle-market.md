<p><strong>The IV Connector Without Needle Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Positive Fluid Displacement</li><li>Negative Fluid Displacement</li><li>Neutral Displacement</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The IV connector without needle market comprises three key types based on fluid displacement mechanisms: </p><p>1. **Positive Fluid Displacement**: This method uses pressure to push fluids through the connector, ensuring efficient delivery of medications. </p><p>2. **Negative Fluid Displacement**: In this system, fluid is drawn back, creating a vacuum to facilitate the flow, promoting safety by minimizing air exposure. </p><p>3. **Neutral Displacement**: This design allows for fluid to move freely without creating pressure or vacuum, reducing the risk of catheter occlusion and enhancing patient comfort. Each type serves specific clinical needs in IV therapy.</p></p>
