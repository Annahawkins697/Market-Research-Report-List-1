<p><p>The PMMA bone cements market is segmented into three main types based on viscosity: low, medium, and high viscosity cements. Low viscosity cements are typically used for applications requiring excellent flow and penetration, such as in minimally invasive surgeries. Medium viscosity cements balance workability and stability, making them suitable for standard orthopedic procedures. High viscosity cements are designed for enhanced stability and resistance to movement, often utilized in challenging anatomical conditions or load-bearing implants, offering superior mechanical properties.</p></p>
