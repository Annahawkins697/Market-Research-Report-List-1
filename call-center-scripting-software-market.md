<p><p>Call center scripting software is categorized primarily into two market types: cloud-based and on-premises. Cloud-based solutions offer flexibility, accessibility, and scalability, allowing agents to access scripts and tools from anywhere with an internet connection. They often feature subscription models and automatic updates. In contrast, on-premises software is installed locally on company servers, providing greater control over data and integration with existing systems. However, it may require significant upfront investment and maintenance, limiting flexibility compared to cloud solutions.</p></p>
