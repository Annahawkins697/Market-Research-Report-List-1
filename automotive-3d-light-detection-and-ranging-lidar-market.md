<p><p>The Automotive 3D Light Detection and Ranging (LiDAR) market is primarily divided into two types: Solid State LiDAR and Mechanical/Scanning LiDAR. Solid State LiDAR employs fixed, integrated components, offering durability and compactness, making it well-suited for mass production and integration in vehicles. In contrast, Mechanical/Scanning LiDAR utilizes rotating mirrors or moving parts to capture a wider field of view, providing high-resolution 3D imaging. Each type serves distinct applications in advanced driver-assistance systems and autonomous driving technologies.</p></p>
