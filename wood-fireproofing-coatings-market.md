<p><strong>The Wood Fireproofing Coatings Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Water-based</li><li>Solvent-based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wood fireproofing coatings market is primarily divided into two main types: water-based and solvent-based coatings. Water-based coatings are eco-friendly, featuring low levels of volatile organic compounds (VOCs), making them safer for indoor applications. They provide good adhesion and flexibility. In contrast, solvent-based coatings typically offer superior fire resistance and durability, making them suitable for harsh environments but may emit higher VOCs. Both types serve to enhance the fire resistance of wood while catering to different environmental and performance needs.</p></p>
