<p><p>The 3D Automated Optical Inspection (AOI) market is divided into two primary types: inline 3D AOI and offline 3D AOI. Inline 3D AOI systems are integrated directly into the production line, enabling real-time inspection of products as they are manufactured, thus minimizing downtime and enhancing efficiency. Conversely, offline 3D AOI systems operate independently of the production line, allowing for batch inspection of completed assemblies. Both types ensure high-quality standards by detecting defects and improving overall production reliability.</p></p>
