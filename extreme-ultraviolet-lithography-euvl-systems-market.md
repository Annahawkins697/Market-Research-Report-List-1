<p><p>Extreme Ultraviolet Lithography (EUVL) systems employ advanced light sources for semiconductor fabrication, operating in the extreme ultraviolet spectrum. The market comprises several types of light sources: </p><p>1. **Laser Produced Plasmas (LPP)** involve high-intensity lasers striking a target material, creating plasmas that emit EUV light. </p><p>2. **Vacuum Sparks** generate EUV radiation through sudden electrical discharges in a vacuum, producing high-temperature plasmas. </p><p>3. **Gas Discharges** harness ionized gases to generate EUV photons, offering an alternative method of emitting extreme ultraviolet radiation for lithography applications.</p></p>
