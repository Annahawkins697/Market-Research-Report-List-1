<p><p>The negative photosensitive materials market is primarily categorized into thermal curing and UV curing types. Thermal curing involves heat activation to harden the material, making it suitable for applications requiring high temperature resistance. In contrast, UV curing utilizes ultraviolet light to initiate the polymerization process, allowing for quick and efficient solidification. These technologies cater to diverse industries such as electronics, automotive, and packaging, where precise formation and durability of components are essential to meet performance demands.</p></p>
