<p><strong>The Angle Measuring Encoder Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Absolute Angle Measuring Encoders</li><li>Incremental Angle Measuring Encoders</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The angle measuring encoder market is primarily divided into two types: absolute and incremental encoders. Absolute angle measuring encoders provide a unique position value for each angle, maintaining positional information even when power is lost, making them ideal for applications requiring high precision and reliability. On the other hand, incremental angle measuring encoders generate signals based on movement or changes in position, requiring a reference point upon startup. This makes them suitable for applications where relative positioning and speed monitoring are essential.</p></p>
