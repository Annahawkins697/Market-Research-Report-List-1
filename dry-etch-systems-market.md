<p><p>The dry etch systems market primarily consists of Reactive Ion Etching (RIE) and Deep Reactive Ion Etching (DRIE) systems. RIE systems utilize plasma to etch materials with high precision, suitable for advanced semiconductor manufacturing and microfabrication. In contrast, DRIE systems offer enhanced etching depth and vertical sidewalls, making them ideal for creating intricate microstructures in MEMS devices and integrated circuits. Both technologies are integral in producing high-performance electronic components, catering to the evolving demands of the semiconductor industry.</p></p>
