<p><p>The Medium Oil Alkyd Resins market is categorized into three main types: </p><p>1. **Dry Alkyd Resins**: These resins contain higher levels of unsaturation, allowing them to cure quickly upon exposure to air, ideal for fast-drying applications.</p><p>2. **Non-Dry Alkyd Resins**: These are primarily used in applications that do not require drying, offering extended working times and flexibility.</p><p>3. **Half Dry Alkyd Resins**: These resins strike a balance, drying slower than dry alkyds but faster than non-dry varieties, suitable for various coatings and finishes.</p></p>
