<p><ul><li>Microphone</li><li>Speaker</li><li>Receiver</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Micro Electronic-Acoustics Market encompasses three primary types: microphones, speakers, and receivers. Microphones convert sound waves into electrical signals, enabling voice and sound capture in devices like smartphones and voice assistants. Speakers, on the other hand, transform electrical signals back into sound, delivering audio output in a variety of devices such as headphones and home audio systems. Receivers are designed to capture and process acoustic signals, often used in telecommunications and hearing aids, ensuring clear audio communication.</p></p>
