<p><p>The industrial applications of enzymes encompass various types, each serving unique functions. Amylase breaks down starches into sugars, essential in food and brewing industries. Lipase aids in fat digestion, useful in detergents and food processing. Protease facilitates protein breakdown, crucial in pharmaceuticals and meat tenderizing. Ligase is involved in DNA manipulation for biotechnology. Phytase enhances phosphorus availability in animal feed. Chordae enzyme plays a role in environmental applications, while xylanase degrades hemicellulose in biofuels and paper production. Each enzyme contributes significantly to efficiency and sustainability across industries.</p></p>
