<p><p>The 3D Automotive Camera Module market consists primarily of two technologies: Complementary Metal Oxide Semiconductor (CMOS) and Charge-Coupled Device (CCD). CMOS cameras are known for their low power consumption, faster processing speeds, and integration capabilities, making them suitable for advanced driver-assistance systems (ADAS). Conversely, CCD cameras offer superior image quality, particularly in low-light conditions, but typically consume more power and are slower. Both types play essential roles in enhancing vehicle safety and enabling autonomous driving features.</p></p>
