<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The DC Isolators Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Break Isolator</li><li>Double Break Isolator</li><li>Pantograph type Isolator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The DC isolators market includes three main types: single break isolators, double break isolators, and pantograph type isolators. Single break isolators feature a single point of interruption for disconnecting circuits, making them suitable for compact installations. Double break isolators provide enhanced safety and reliability by utilizing two break points, often preferred in high-voltage applications. Pantograph type isolators are designed for overhead lines, facilitating easy disconnection in railway systems and similar setups, allowing efficient maintenance and operational safety.</p></p>
